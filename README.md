# ecohero

The name of this app is EcoHero. It is an app dedicated towards raising environmental awareness in local communities. It includes sustainability games made by the community, an AI-powered waste sorter, and tips on how to stay eco-friendly! To use the app, you must first sign up by making a username and password. Do not forget your login credentials as they will be necessary everytime you login! After that, you will be directed to the home page where you can explore the different things the app has to offer. From there, things will be self-explanatory.
